During my time at Warner Bros. I had completed an unrelated AR project just immediately prior to the start of my internship. Due to my experience with Unity I was able to hop on this project and create some renderings for the ride.

WHAT IS?

These are concepts for an Aquaman component of the VR ride and were created with the rider's journey in mind. This project became an exercise in visualization and utilizing my engineering skillset to inform my design decisions.

I learned alot from this unique opportunity about conceptualizing environments and experiences. Most importantly, I learned how to focus on human-centered design, while also pushing the boundaries of the content I was given.
